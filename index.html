中文

本应用（MFA Authenticator）重视您的隐私。本政策说明我们如何处理信息。

1. 我们收集的信息

  • 相机权限（android.permission.CAMERA）：仅用于扫描二维码（如 OTP 配置码）。
  • 账号与密钥数据：您添加的 MFA/TOTP 信息仅保存在您的设备本地。

2. 我们如何使用信息

  • 相机仅在扫码页面启用，用于识别二维码内容。
  • 识别出的信息用于在本地生成动态验证码。

3. 数据存储与安全

  • 数据默认存储于本地设备，不会上传到我们的服务器。
  • 应用使用系统安全能力（如安全存储）保护敏感数据。

4. 数据共享

我们不会将您的 MFA 账号、密钥或二维码内容出售或共享给第三方。

5. 您的控制权

  • 您可以随时删除应用中的账号数据。
  • 卸载应用后，本地应用数据会随系统机制被移除（具体取决于设备系统行为）。

6. 儿童隐私

本应用不以 13 岁以下儿童为目标人群，也不会主动收集儿童个人信息。

7. 政策更新

我们可能更新本政策。更新后会在本页面显示最新日期。

8. 联系方式

English

This app (MFA Authenticator) respects your privacy. This policy explains how information is handled.

1. Information We Process

  • Camera permission (android.permission.CAMERA): used only to scan QR codes (e.g., OTP setup codes).
  • Account and secret data: MFA/TOTP data you add is stored locally on your device.

2. How We Use Information

  • The camera is activated only on scanning screens to read QR code content.
  • Scanned data is used locally to generate one-time passcodes.

3. Storage and Security

  • Data is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to our servers.
  • The app uses platform security features (such as secure storage) to protect sensitive data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your MFA accounts, secrets, or QR content with third parties.

5. Your Choices

  • You can remove account data in the app at any time.
  • When the app is uninstalled, local app data is removed according to device OS behavior.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect children's personal information.

7. Policy Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest date will be shown on this page.
